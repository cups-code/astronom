#Maths #Algebra 

> [!Info] Explanation
> To simplify algebraic expressions, follow below.
> $$a + a = 2a$$
> $$a - a = 0$$
> $$a \times a = a^2$$
> $$a \times b = ab$$
> $$a \div b = \frac{a}{b}$$

> [!Example] Application
> $$7a + 3a = 10a$$
> $$4a \times 4b = 16ab$$
> $$8a \div 8b = \frac{a}{b}$$
