#Science #Chemistry

- A synthesis reaction is a form of reaction that involves two reactants forming a compound.
- It is the opposite of a decomposition reaction.

> [!Info] Formula
> $$\ce{A + B -> AB}$$

> [!Example] Application
> $$\ce{Hydrogen Gas + Oxygen -> Water}$$
> $$\ce{2H_2_{(g)} + O_2_{(g)} -> 2H_2O_{(l)}}$$
