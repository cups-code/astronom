> [!Info] Formula
> $$\text{Energy Efficiency} = \frac{\text{Useful Energy}}{\text{Total Input}} \times 100$$

> [!Example] Application
> - Total Input = 60J
> - Waste = 20J
> - Useful Input = 60 - 20 = 40J
> $$ \text{E} = \frac{40}{60} \times 100 $$
> $$ \therefore \text{E} = 66.\overline{6} \space \text{(1 d. p.)} $$
